Is this real footage or AI?+
100% AI-generated. The product photos you upload anchor the look; the people, scenes, and motion are generated by Higgsfield's seedance_2_0 model and tuned by hand. Most viewers can't tell on a TikTok feed — and that's exactly why hook-rate holds up against shot-on-camera creative.
Can I see more samples?+
The three Northwave ads above are the full Launch Pack you'd receive — same number of files, same finish. We'll show 2–3 more examples on the fit call if you're seriously evaluating. We don't have a public portfolio yet because every brand we work with is exclusive in its category — your competitor doesn't get to see your work.
What if I hate the ads?+
The storyboard gate at day 1 is where you redirect cheaply — change direction, swap concepts, kill a shot. After delivery, the hook-rate guarantee covers you: if the ads don't outperform your current creative in a 7-day A/B test, full refund. We absorb the cost.
Can you rush a job?+
3-day rush available on the Launch Pack for +£200, subject to slot availability. Ask on the fit call. We don't compress the storyboard gate — that's the cheapest moment to redirect, so we keep the full 24 hours there even on rush jobs.
Who writes the ad copy?+
You do. We shoot what you give us — your headline, your CTA, your voice. If you want help, we can recommend two copywriters we trust who specialise in DTC paid social. They're not on our payroll; you'd pay them directly.
